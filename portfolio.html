Hi, this is my portfolio

In a recent effort to document all my progress on projects, I have started detailing my work on Github. Projects on my Github (should) have a very detailed account of the development process. Past projects did not receive this treatment, however I am more than happy to answer any questions you have about them!

Electric guitar

This was an electric guitar I built based on the classic stratocaster model, with 3 mini humbuckers and all austrailian timber. It uses a Tasmanian blackwood body and QLD Maple neck.

Guitar Amplifier

I built this amp to emulate the amp of my then guitar idol SRV, the classic Fender Vibroverb. I developed a faithful recreation with a few select modifications such as modern capacitors, resistors, an effects loop and a custom housing.

Acoustic guitar

This was an acoustic guitar I built entirely from scratch based on the classic Martin dreadnought body shape and bracing pattern. It uses a tasmanian blackwood top, back and sides, as well as a queensland maple neck.

Business card

I developed these business cards as an eye catching way to stand out from the crowd while immediately demonstrating my engineering skills. The card pictured uses the RP2040 microcontroller and WS2812b addressable LEDs.
